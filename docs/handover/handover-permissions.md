# Handover Permissions

Capabilities:

- `handover.view`
- `handover.create`
- `handover.acknowledge`
- `handover.resolve`
- `handover.carry_forward`
- `handover.view_history`
- `handover.manage`

Visibility follows nursing home, ward, selected shift, operational date and the user's role/capabilities. Cross-home and cross-ward leakage is prohibited.
