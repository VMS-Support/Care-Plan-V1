## Existing queue and count locations

- Upcoming Care Interventions and Next 4 Hours: `src/components/operations/OperationsHub.tsx`, backed by `intervention-schedule.ts` and the Phase 19 `dueTime.ts` classifier.
- Task counts and Assigned To Me-style filtering: `TaskWorkflowEngine.tsx`, `AppShell.tsx`, operational-context task scoping.
- Assessment due/overdue counts: `assessments.index.tsx`, `assessments.reassessment.tsx`.
- Observation status/compliance: Vitals and observation components plus observation schedules.
- Handover ward/shift/user acknowledgement: `handoverContext.ts` and `handovers.tsx`.

## Shared foundations and indexes

Home/ward selection is centralised in `operationalContext.ts`; due classification in `dueTime.ts`; audit in `auditFramework.ts`; events in `src/domain/events`; and rule replay in `src/domain/rules`. No database indexes exist. For a future database, implement the unique source key and indexes documented in `unified-work-item-model.md`.

## Current status values discovered

- Task: pending, in progress, completed, overdue, deleted.
- Care Action definition: active, review due, completed, cancelled, superseded, discontinued; occurrence outcomes completed, partially completed, missed, refused, escalated.
- Assessment: draft/workflow/completed/approved plus archive/delete/version lifecycle.
- Care plan: draft, active, review/evaluation due and overdue, completed, superseded, archived, inactive.
- Handover: legacy active/read/acknowledged/open/completed/closed/archived, normalised to active, acknowledged, resolved, expired, cancelled.
- Central due classifier: upcoming, next hour, next four hours, due this shift, due today, due now, overdue, missed, completed on time/late, deferred, cancelled, not applicable, discontinued.

These values remain source-specific. Adapters map them into the canonical model; source enums are not overwritten.
