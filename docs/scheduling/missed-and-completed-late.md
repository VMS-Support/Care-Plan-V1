# Missed And Completed Late

Missed is a workflow status, not a synonym for very overdue. A missed transition must be explicit or produced by an approved background rule with reason, actor or system rule, effective time and audit trail.

Completed late uses the effective clinical completion time where available. If care occurred at 10:05 but documentation was entered at 10:30, the lateness calculation uses 10:05 and audit/history retain the entry time separately.
