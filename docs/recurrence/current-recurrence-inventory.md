# Current Recurrence Inventory

Discovery date: 2026-07-13. This repository is a React/local-storage application. It has no cron scheduler, durable background worker, database occurrence table, or database uniqueness constraint.

| Area                            | Current implementation                                                            | Behaviour                                                                                                                                            | Gaps and migration risk                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               |
| ------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Care Actions                    | `src/lib/care/intervention-schedule.ts`, `ProblemIntervention.frequencyType`      | Converts hourly, 2/4/6-hourly, daily, twice/three-times daily, per-shift, weekly and monthly into minutes. The latest log anchors one next due time. | It does not materialise distinct occurrences. Monthly is treated as 30 days, per-shift as 8 hours, and `endOfCurrentShift` has hard-coded 06:00/14:00/22:00 boundaries. Protected Upcoming/Next 4 Hours behaviour means shadow parity is required before replacement. |
| Care Action recurrence metadata | `ProblemIntervention` in `types.ts`                                               | once, per shift, daily, twice/three-times daily, weekly, monthly, PRN, 1/2/4/6-hourly, custom                                                        | Custom meaning is partly free-form. Start/review/end dates exist. Cancellation/discontinuation fields exist on the definition.                                                                                                                                        |
| Assessment reminders            | Assessment routes, `Assessment` dates                                             | `dueDate`, `reviewDate`, `nextReassessmentDate`, and review frequency drive page-level due/reassessment views.                                       | No stable reminder occurrence entity or shared generator. Date comparisons are distributed across assessment screens.                                                                                                                                                 |
| Observation schedules           | `ObservationPlan`, `ObservationSchedule`, observation UI/store                    | Required items carry 4/8/12-hourly, daily, weekly, monthly, or PRN frequency. Completed observations are persisted evidence.                         | No durable reminder occurrence generation. Compliance derives from records and schedule metadata.                                                                                                                                                                     |
| Tasks                           | `Task.recurrence`, `TaskWorkflowEngine.tsx`                                       | none/daily/weekly/monthly/custom metadata and a reminder timestamp are captured.                                                                     | Recurrence does not generate stable Task occurrences. Custom recurrence is free text and cannot be safely parsed without user reconciliation.                                                                                                                         |
| Appointments                    | Appointment fields embedded in Task                                               | Appointment-like Tasks use Task due date/time.                                                                                                       | No standalone Appointment source or recurrence model; must remain a Task until such a module exists.                                                                                                                                                                  |
| Care-plan review                | `CarePlan.reviewDate/evaluationDate`, `CarePlanProblem.reviewDate/evaluationDate` | Page-level quality and overdue calculations.                                                                                                         | No shared generator or stable occurrence. Formal review records remain completion evidence.                                                                                                                                                                           |
| Handovers                       | `handoverContext.ts`                                                              | Shift targeting uses configured Shift IDs and effective/expiry windows; acknowledgement is user-specific.                                            | Handover creation is event/manual, not recurring. Future recurrence is contract-only.                                                                                                                                                                                 |
| Next 4 Hours / Upcoming         | `OperationsHub.tsx`, `intervention-schedule.ts`, `dueTime.ts`                     | Active intervention definitions are reduced to one next due item, scoped by ward/user and centrally classified where operational context is present. | Exact item/order/label parity must be measured before canonical cutover.                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| Due classification              | `dueTime.ts`                                                                      | Central due-now/overdue/shift/day classification uses operational context timezone.                                                                  | It classifies supplied due times but does not generate them.                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| Shift calculations              | `operationalContext.ts`                                                           | Home-specific Shift Definitions and stable IDs are available.                                                                                        | Some conversion uses the host process timezone; the canonical recurrence engine resolves shift starts explicitly in the nursing-home timezone.                                                                                                                        |
| PRN                             | Care Action and observation frequency values                                      | Existing intervention minute conversion returns no schedule for PRN.                                                                                 | No explicit trigger occurrence/reason contract previously existed.                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| Events and rules                | `src/domain/events`, `src/domain/rules`                                           | Events have IDs, home/timezone scope, idempotent receipts and rule replay.                                                                           | No shared event-to-occurrence scheduling service existed.                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| Duplicate prevention            | Event receipts, Work source keys                                                  | Events and Work projections have idempotency foundations.                                                                                            | Scheduled occurrences previously lacked deterministic IDs.                                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| Timezone and DST                | operational context, `Intl` in due classifier                                     | Europe/Dublin defaults and local-day due classification exist.                                                                                       | No documented recurrence policy for missing or duplicate wall-clock hours.                                                                                                                                                                                            |
| Future horizon                  | none                                                                              | Existing screens derive the next item on demand.                                                                                                     | No bounded materialisation policy. Canonical default is 30 days.                                                                                                                                                                                                      |
| Resident/source cancellation    | lifecycle helpers and source status fields                                        | Discharged/deceased residents are excluded from bedside context; definitions may be cancelled/discontinued.                                          | Future occurrences were not available to suspend/cancel while retaining completed history.                                                                                                                                                                            |

## Parallel-run decision

The canonical engine is additive. `legacyScheduleAdapters.ts` translates current Care Action, Task, Assessment, and Observation schedule metadata into `RecurrenceRule`. Existing Operations reads remain authoritative until stable source-occurrence comparison proves the same item set, order, due times, ward scope, and completion linkage.
