# Audit Reason Policy

`requiresAuditReason(action, entityType)` centralises high-impact reason requirements.

Reasons are required for voids, corrections, deletes, inactivation, discontinued care actions, dismissed clinical alerts, deferred overdue work, cancelled appointments, discharge, deceased marking, effective-date corrections, role/permission changes, temporary ward overrides, cross-home corrections and document replacement/removal.

Routine low-risk updates, such as a non-sensitive contact correction, do not require a reason unless policy changes later.
