# Current Role Capability Matrix

Only roles already present in the repository are activated as templates: `DON`, `CNM`, `NURSE`, `DOCTOR` and `HCA`.

| Role template | Scope baseline | Current migrated capabilities |
|---|---|---|
| DON | Assigned nursing home, management ward scope | Residents, assessments, care plans, incidents, reports, audit, compliance, observations, operations and governance-oriented controls currently available to DON. |
| CNM | Assigned nursing home and management ward scope | Residents, assessments, care plans, incidents, reports, compliance, observations and operations currently available to CNM. |
| NURSE | Assigned home and approved wards | Resident care, assessments, care plans, incidents, observations and own operational records currently available to Nurse. |
| DOCTOR | Assigned home and approved clinical scope | Clinical view, MDT notes, medical review, treatment notes, observations and care-plan viewing currently available to Doctor. |
| HCA | Assigned home and approved wards | Resident viewing, daily notes, interventions, handovers, observations and own operational records currently available to Carer/HCA. |

Future role keys are documented only: Allied Health, Maintenance, Housekeeping, HR, Training Manager, Admin, Finance, Owner, Group Executive, Family and Pharmacy.
